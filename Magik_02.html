Magik's Abilities

Soulsword Strike

Magik's primary melee attack with her mystical Soulsword, dealing moderate damage with quick slashes.

Limbo Portal

Create temporary portals that allies and enemies can use for strategic positioning.

Dimensional Shift

Teleport short distances to evade attacks or reposition during combat.

Darkchylde's Grasp

Summon demonic hands from Limbo to immobilize enemies in an area.

Gameplay Tips

  • Use portals to create escape routes for your team or cut off enemy retreats
  • Combine Dimensional Shift with Soulsword attacks for hit-and-run tactics
  • Save Darkchylde's Grasp for when enemies are grouped together
  • Practice portal placement to master battlefield control
  • Use your teleport to reach high ground and gain tactical advantage

Counters & Matchups

  • Strong against: Slow, ground-based heroes without mobility options
  • Weak against: Heroes with silence abilities or long-range snipers
  • Pair well with tanky heroes who can protect you while you set up portals
  • Avoid direct confrontations with brawlers - use your mobility instead
